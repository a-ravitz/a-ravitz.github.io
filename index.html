About Me

Summer of 2005, I moved to Philadelphia to study music at the University of The Arts. Since school I have had the opportunity to play hundred's of shows, but my most note worthy musical accomplishment has been touring with one of my grandfather's favorites, the Glenn Miller Orchestra.


When I stopped touring with the GMO in 2011, I began working in bars. I've had every job imaginable, from barbacking and changing kegs, to managing the team, creating drinks, and serving alongside some of the city's best bartenders at Franklin Mortgage and Investment, Co. In 2016 I took another step deeper into the booze business by becoming a brand ambassador for Louisville Distilling, Co., representing Angel's Envy Bourbon.


When I'm not working, playing bass, cooking, or riding my bike, I'm creating innovative web apps, and honing my skills as a full stack web developer.
